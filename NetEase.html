NetEase Games Internship

"Passionate for Games"

Brief

Intern at NetEase Games as Experience Design Intern, I worked on two projects: Onmyoji and an unannounced project. My tasks includes optimizing interaction flows in game systems, desigining interfaces for ongoing games, building prototypes using Unity/C# and pitching solutions to producers. Due to the NDA, I am not able to disclose work details of the projects, but I would like to talk about the experience and what I learned.

Lessons

1) How to immerse players in virtual world, providing emotionally engaging experience through interaction and interface design. 2) How to leverage motion design to lower players' cognitive load, expand screen space on mobile devices and provide players with surprises. 3) Making trade off between massive information in MMORPG and immersive experience in virtual reality. 4) Interfaces design for virtual reality, considering 3D space, human factors and emotional design.
