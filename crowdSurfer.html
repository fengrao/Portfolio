About The Project

- Time: Jan. 2017 (48 hours)
- Platform: PC
- Tools: Maya, Photoshop
- Team Size: 6 (Two programmers, two artists and one sound designer)
- My Role: Game Designer, 3D Artist

My Contributions

- Designed game mechanics
- Designed interaction
- Designed 3D assets using Maya and Adobe Creatives.

Crowd Surfer is a rhythm game designed and implemented within 48 hours in 2017 global game jam. It received the Jammer's Choice Award. I worked as game designer, 3D modeler in a team of 6. My contribution includes coming up with the idea, designing interaction with the crowd, in order to control the surfer to fit in the rhythm.
