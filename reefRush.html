Reef Rush

Introduction


The World HD is an MMOARPG, hitting top 3 in China App Store Top Charts in 2014. Though it has excellent gameplay design, the user experience is very disappointing. The redesign of Summoned Monsters System is one of my important works during my internship in Netease.

My Role


UX Designer (In User Experience Center)
- Needs analysis
- Problems framing
- Low fidelity prototype design

Problem Analysis

Redundant Procedures


There are many redundant procedures making upgrading equipment inconvenient.

Low-Efficient Access


The system did not provide efficient access to different monsters and their equipment. Players have to constantly go back to the first level interface in order to switch to other monsters and their equipment .

Lack of Consistency


Lack of consistency leads to players’ confusion and misoperation.

Design Challenges

Development Issues


Since the game has already been developed for two years and shipped in App Store, the redesign is not allowed to be greatly changed from the original version. I have to take the cost of development, programming issues and users habits into consideration.

Trade-off


Since the World HD is an MMORPG, there are many different complex systems in the game. The requirement of redesign is to deal with overwhelming information, but also keep the functionality complete at the same time. Designers have to make a trade-off between these factors.

Solution & Design

Upgrade All


To upgrade monsters' equipment, players have to update each piece of equipment one by one in preivous version, which is of low efficiency. Based on the original system and interface, I added a “upgrade all equipment” button by deleting the redundant monster's portrait and moving experience bar to the left. In this case, players are able to upgrade all the equipment at once rather than upgrading them one by one.

Switch Arrows


I provided left and right switching arrows at the edge of the interfaces, allowing players to switch from one monster/ equipment to another without going back to the first level interface.

Upgrade To Maximum


In the mid-late phrase of the game, when players get newly-gained equipment with higher attributes, they will update it to maximum levels. Since upgrading the equipment from 0 level to maximum level will take a lot of time, I changed the button “Upgrading five times” into “Upgrading to maximum” to guarantee the highest efficiency.
