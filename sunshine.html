Introduction


Afternoon Sunshine is an individual work of a course New Media And User Interface Design. The icons are designed to create an impression of warmth and brightness.

Graphic / UI Design

Theme


The afternoon sunshine usually reminds me of a period of time in my high school. I usually sat on the chair in my balcony and enjoyed the sunshine after lunch, snatching a little leisure from the overwhelming study. The afternoon sunshine becomes a memory of my leisure time. Inspired by my previous experience, I want to use afternoon sunshine as a theme to design a series of icons that provoke people's such feelings.

Flat Design


Functionality & Identity


An important principle in design is that form follows function. The colorful and pretty outlooks should not overshadow icon's functionality. Since the icon's significance lies in its identity, I designed icons with elements that can help users easily distinguish them. The concrete elements help users link abstract concepts to specific functions. For instance, the icon "cloud" refers to "weather", the icon "mail" refers to "message".
