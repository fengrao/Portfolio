Bouncing Bullet War is a competitive two-player third person shooting game. Each player controls a character shooting bouncing bullets. The bullet will change its direction when it hits the wall. If the player defenses the bullet successfully, the bullet will be bounced back and turned into his/her bullet. Players have to make full use of the bouncing mechanic and the defense action to defeat the opponent.



Added defense mechanics to increase skills.



Defense limit prevents player negative defense.



I added character attributes to solve some problem. The hit point increase the gaming time. The power requires players make wise use of bullets. The defense limit prevents players negative defense and encourages them to attack actively.
